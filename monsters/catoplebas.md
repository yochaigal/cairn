# Catoplebas
7 HP, 13 STR, tail(d8)  
- Monstrous creatures with a long, bony tail, a bloated body, short and stumpy legs, a long neck, and a boar-like head.   Dwell in swamps.  
- Critical damage: the tail renders the victim stunned (d8 DEX damage).
