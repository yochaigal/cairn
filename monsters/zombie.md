# Zombie
2 HP, 14 STR, 6 DEX, 3 WIL, weapon (d8)
-  Slow, mindless humanoid corpses, reanimated as guardians by  powerful clerics or wizards.
-   Attack anything that comes nearby.
-   Immune to any mind affect effects
