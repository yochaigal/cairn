# Crocodile, Large
6 HP, 1 Armor, 14 STR, 8 DEX, bite (d8)  
• 20’ long, may attack small boats and canoes  
• Attracted to the scent of blood or heavy movement in the water  
• Swim silently just under the surface to ambush victims
