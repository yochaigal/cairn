## Barebones Gear Packages

### Fighter

#### Starting Gear

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* **Torches** (3 uses)
* **Sword** (d8)
* **Shield** (+1 Armor)
* **Gambeson** (+1 Armor)
* **Bandages** (3 uses)
* Token from a fallen comrade (_petty_)

### Thief

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* Torches (3 uses)
* **Lockpick** 
* **Grappling Hook**  
* **Cloak** (_petty_) 
* **Lantern**
* **Dagger** (d6)
* **Oil Can** (6 uses)

### Magic-User

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* **Torches** (3 uses)
* **Spellbook** (roll d100 on [Spellbooks](/barebones/barebones-character-creation/#spellbooks) table)
* **Dagger** (d6)
* **Chalk (*petty*)**
* **Parchment** (3 uses)
* **Robes** (_petty_)

### Cleric

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* **Torches** (3 uses)
* **Mace** (d8)
* **Holy Symbol** (_petty_)
* **Gambeson** (1 Armor)
* **Bandages** (3 uses)
