## Barebones Gear Packages

### Fighter

#### Starting Gear

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* **Torches** (3 uses)
* **Sword** (d8)
* **Shield** (+1 Armor)
* **Gambeson** (+1 Armor)
* **Bandages** (3 uses)
* Token from a fallen comrade (*petty*)

### Thief

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* **Lantern**
* **Oil Can (6 uses)**
* **Dagger** (d6)
* **Sling** (d6)
* **Thieving Tools** (Lockpick, Metal File, etc.)
* **Rope (25ft)**
* Black Hood (_petty_)

### Magic-User

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* **Spellbook** (roll d100 on [Spellbooks](/barebones/barebones-character-creation/#spellbooks) table)
* **Dagger** (d6)
* **Torches** (3 uses)
* **Chalk (*petty*)** and **Parchment (3 uses)**
* **Robes** (_petty_)

### Cleric

* 3d6 Gold Pieces
* Rations (3 uses)
* **Mace** (d8)
* **Holy Symbol (*petty*)**
* **Gambeson** (1 Armor)
* **Bandages** (3 uses)
