## **The Skitterlord**

8 HP, 1 Armor, 14 STR, 12 DEX, 15 WIL, kick (d6)

* **Vomit Insects:** DEX save or take d8 damage. Nearby Insect Swarms increases their STR with d8. *This ability may only be used 3x/day.*  
* **Summon Insects:** Call forth an Insect Swarm or d3 Fire Beatles. Arrives in d2 turns.  
* **Swarm Form:** Can transform into a swarm of beetles.  
* **Swarm Step:** May teleport through insect swarms within 2 miles.
