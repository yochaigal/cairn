# Air Elemental

16 HP, 11 STR, 15 DEX, 8 WIL, _detachment_

- Living vortexes of whirling air and gas. Lighter creatures are swept away by their mere presence.
- **Whirlwind**: All nearby targets that fail a STR save are blown away.
