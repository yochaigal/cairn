# Hippogriff

3 HP, 12 STR, 14 DEX, claws (d6+d6) or bite (d10)

- Hybrid creatures with the head and front half of an eagle and the back half of a horse.
- Can be trained to be used as mounts.
- Hate pegasi, attacking then on sight.
