# Giant Centipede

5 HP, 6 STR, 13 DEX, 3 WIL, sting (d6)

- Dog-sized centipedes that live in damp, underground places. Avoid combat if possible. 
- **Critical Damage**: Venom renders the target _deprived_. Save STR once per day to recover.
