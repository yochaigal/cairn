#  Hirelings

Below are example hirelings for use in your Cairn campaign.

## Alchemist (30/day)

2 HP, 8 STR, 10 DEX, 14 WIL

- Rations (3 uses)
- Torch (3 uses)
- Dagger (d6)
- Acid
- Lens
- Gloves (_petty_)

## Animal Handler (5/day)

3 HP, 9 STR, 14 DEX, 12 WIL

- Rations (3 uses)
- Torch (3 uses)
- Dagger (d6)
- Cage
- Rope (25ft)
- Hawk [2 HP, 5 STR, 16 DEX, 5 WIL, claws (d4+d4)]
