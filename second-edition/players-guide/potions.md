# Potions

A d100 list of potions that may be brewed.

1. Ageing Potion - A potion that, depending on the amount taken, temporarily ages the drinker to various ages.
1. Alihotsy Draught - A potion from the Alihotsy plant; causes hysterical laughter.
1. Amortentia - The world's strongest Love Potion; does not create real love, just powerful obsession.
1. Anti-Paralysis Potion - A potion that heals paralysis.
1. Antidote to Common Poisons - A potion that reverses the effects of common poisons. Contains Bezoar, Mistletoe Berries, and Unicorn Horns.
1. Antidote to Veritaserum - A potion that removes the effects of Veritaserum.
1. Babbling Beverage - A potion that causes uncontrollable speaking of nonsense.
1. Baruffio's Brain Elixir - A potion that apparently increases the taker's brain power. +1d4 WIL for one skill check (3 uses).
1. Beautification Potion - A potion that makes the taker very beautiful.
1. Befuddlement Draught - A potion that makes the taker confused and reckless for 1d4 rounds.
1. Blood-Replenishing Potion - A potion that replenishes the taker's blood if they have been injured and lost blood.
1. Bloodroot Poison - A poisonous potion.
1. Burn-Healing Paste - A thick paste used to heal burns.
1. Calming Draught - A potion that calms the user of shock, trauma, etc.
1. Caxambu Style Borborygmus Potion - Provokes stomach growling.
1. Confidence boosting brew - A potion that made the drinker more confident.
1. Cough Potion - A potion that soothes and reduces coughs.
1. Cure for Boil -  A potion that cures boils.
1. Deflating Draught- A potion that deflates anything swollen by magical means.
1. Developing Solution - A potion used in the developing of magical photographs, making them move.
1. Dizziness Draught - A potion that makes the taker dizzy and lightheaded.
1. Dogbreath Potion - Gives the drinker a fiery breath. Has a purple hue.
1. Draught of Living Death - A potion that places the taker in a state of sleep that makes them seem like they are dead.
1. Draught of Peace - A potion that relieves anxiety.
1. Dreamless Sleep Potion - A potion that places the taker in a sleep that is dreamless.
1. Drink of Despair - A potion that puts the taker in extreme pain.
1. Drowsiness Draught - Makes drinker drowsy.
1. Edurus Potion - A potion that made a rocky film develop on the drinker's skin, increasing their defence and helping to protect them from injury.
1. Elixir of Life - A potion created from the Philosopher's Stone that extend the taker's life.
1. Elixir to Induce Euphoria - A potion that induces a sense of inexplicable, irrational happiness upon the drinker.
1. Emerald Potion (Also knwown as the Drink of Despair) - A potion that protects powerfully magic objects, such as horcruxes. It cannot be touched, magically vanished or emptied or changed. When drunk it causes severe pain, the guts feel like burning, the drinker will remember his or her most awful experiences, and finally, causes extreme thirst.
1. Erumpent Potion - A potion that is highly explosive when it touches or is touched by an outside source.
1. Everklena - A supposed cleaning product that in reality caused grime to spawn.
1. Essence of Insanity - A potion which presumably causes irrational behaviour, possibly inflicting the condition of insanity.
1. Everlasting Elixirs - A potion with the effect to either never run out of potion or to work forever.
1. Exploding Potion - A very volatile potion, and when completed, can be used to create explosions.
1. Exstimulo Potion - Boosts the spell power of the drinker.
1. Fake Protective Potions - A potion that claimed to protect the taker from Dark magic, but did not.
1. Fatiguing Infusion - A potion that, when either drank or inhaled, caused fatigue.
1. Felix Felicis (Also known as Liquid Luck) - A potion that temporarily made the drinker lucky, allowing them to succeed in all their endeavours.
1. Fergus Fungal Budge - A fungicidal product that treated ringworm infections on the feet.
1. Fire-Breathing Potion - was a dangerous potion that could make a person breathe fire
1. Fire Protection Potion - A potion that protects the taker from being burnt.
1. Flesh-Eating Slug Repellent - A pesticide that repels flesh-eating slugs.
1. Focus Potion - Reduced the cooldown time between spells.
1. Forgetfulness Potion - A potion that makes the taker forgetful.
1. Fungiface Potion - A potion that makes the taker's face break out in fungi.
1. Garrotting Gas - A gas that induces choking and possibly suffocates the taker.
1. Germinating Potion - A potion that helps to grow plants.
1. Girding Potion - A potion that gives the taker extra endurance.
1. Grand Pepperup Potion - A stronger version of Pepperup Potion.
1. Gregory's Unctuous Unction - A potion that causes the drinker to believe that whomever gave the potion to them is their best friend.
1. Hair-Dyeing Potion - A potion that changes the taker's hair colour.
1. Hair-Raising Potion - A potion that causes the taker's hair to stand on end.
1. Hate Potion - A potion that shows the taker's worst traits and habits.
1. Healing Potion - A potion which heals the drinker, it replenishes any lost health or stamina
1. Herbicide Potion - A potion that either kills or damages plants. Contains Flobberworm mucus, Horclump juice, and Spines of lionfish.
1. Invigoration Draught - A potion that is presumed to give the taker an energy boost.
1. Invisibility Potion - A potion that makes the taker invisible.
1. Jawbind Potion - A potion that induces lockjaw. 
1. Laugh-inducing Potion - A potion that makes the taker laugh uncontrollably.
1. Love Potion - Any of a large number of potions that make the taker fall in love with the person that has given it to them.
1. Love Potion Antidote - Antidote to love potions.
1. Madame Glossy's Silver Polish - A magical cleaning solution.
1. Mandrake Restorative Draught - A potion made from Mandrakes that restores a person that has been petrified to their original state.
1. Manegro Potion - Causes hair on drinker's head to grow out rapidly
1. Maxima Potion - Temporarily makes the drinker's spells more powerful.
1. Maximum Turbo Farts Potion - Presumably causes the drinker to rapidly develop flatulence
1. Mrs Scower's All-Purpose Magical Mess Remover - Removes stains.
1. Murtlap Essence - Soothes and heals painful cuts and abrasions.
1. Noxious Poison - A poison that releases green clouds of smoke when completed.
1. Oculus Potion - Restores the drinker's sight. Counteracts the Conjunctivitis Curse
1. Pepperup Potion - Relieves and/or cures cold symptoms.
1. Polyjuice Potion - Temporarily transforms the drinker into another person. The drinker will take on the appearance of the person whose hair, fingernails, etc., are added to the potion. Not to be used for transforming into an animal.
1. Pompion Potion - Temporarily turns the drinker's head into a pumpkin.
1. Potion of All Potential - Brings out the drinker's best qualities, unlocking their full potential and enhancing their prowess of accomplishment. Increase highest stat by 1d4 for skill check or combat (1 use).
1. Potion of Invisibility - Temporarily turns the drinker invisible.
1. Regeneration Potion - Helps restore non-corporeal wizards to their bodies.
1. Regerminating Potion - Forces the germination of a plant
1. Restoration Potion - Reverts spell effects.
1. Revive Potion - Awakens an unconscious person.
1. Rudimentary Body Potion - Helps restore non-corporeal wizards to a rudimentary bodies/sustains rudimentary bodies.
1. Scintillation Solution - Capable of making objects brilliantly shiny.
1. Shrinking Solution - Causes the drinker to shrink.
1. Skele-Gro - Regrows missing bones.
1. Sleeping Draught - Made the drinker quickly fall into a deep but temporary sleep.
1. Star Grass Salve - Healing potion for soothing injuries.
1. Strengthening Solution - Increases the strength of the one who drinks it. Increase 1d4 STR for skill check or combat (1 use).
1. Stupid Potion - Caused a loss of intelligence. Reduce 1d4 STR for skill check or combat (1 use).
1. Swelling Solution - Causes enlargement on contact.
1. Thunderbrew - Surrounds the drinker's body with a miniature thunderstorm that zaps enemies.
1. Tonic for Trace Detection - Allows the drinker to sense traces of magic better than they normally could.
1. Venomous Tentacula Juice - Acts like a (non-fatal) poison.
1. Veritaserum - Forces the taker to tell the truth.
1. Volubilis Potion - Alters the drinker's voice
1. Weakness Potion - Weakens the drinker. Reduce 1d4 STR for skill check or combat (1 use).
1. Wideye Potion - Prevents the drinker from falling asleep. Also awakens from drugging or concussion.
1. Wiggenweld Potion - Able to sterilise and heal minor wounds.
1. Wit-Sharpening Potion - Presumably enhances the clarity of thought of the drinker
1. Wolfsbane Potion - Eases the symptoms of lycanthropy; prevents werewolves from losing their minds post-transformation.
